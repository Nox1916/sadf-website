Trust • Experience • Growth About SADF

Shriram Agrotech Development Farm (SADF) is a trusted agricultural seed production business with 25 years of strong farmer relationships and 20 years of seed production experience.

Over the years, SADF has developed a reliable network of farmers and field-level expertise that supports large-scale seed production activities.

We specialize in bulk seed production, certified wheat seed production, and research wheat seed production while maintaining a commitment to quality, consistency, and agricultural reliability.

At SADF, we believe that successful agriculture begins with trust, strong farmer relationships, and responsible seed production practices.
